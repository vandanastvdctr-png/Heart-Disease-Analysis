# Heart-Disease-Analysis
Learning how to use GitHub
The Heart Disease Analysis project uses Tableau to convert patient health data into simple charts and dashboards. It analyzes information such as age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol, heart rate, and chest pain to understand the factors that may lead to heart disease.

With Tableau, it becomes easy to identify patterns, compare different patient groups, and find people who may be at a higher risk of heart disease. The interactive dashboard helps users understand the data quickly and supports better healthcare decisions.


Tools Used:

Tableau Desktop,
MySQL,
Heart Disease Dataset
